How Revenue Streams Work
The management company earns revenue through two types of fees charged to each property:

Service Fees (Base Management Fee) — a set of named service categories, each charged as a percentage of the property's Total Revenue. The sum of all active service categories equals the total Base Management Fee.

Incentive Fee — a performance bonus charged as a percentage of Gross Operating Profit (GOP), only collected when GOP is positive.

Together, these fees are deducted from NOI in the USALI waterfall: Revenue → GOP → Fixed Charges → NOI → Mgmt Fees → FF&E → ANOI. Values set here are defaults — each property can override them individually.
