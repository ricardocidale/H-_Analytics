6. Ambiguity & Reconstruction Risk Flags
The slide is ~70% raster. Every dark band, every divider line, every bullet dot, every dark card background is a baked-in PNG/JPG. A rebuilder who tries to recreate from this spec without the source rasters will get the text right but the visual chrome wrong.
No theme colors used — every color is hardcoded sRGB. Preserve hex exactly.
Fonts: Poppins ExtraLight is custom (not stock with PowerPoint), Georgia is system, Microsoft YaHei is Windows-only (will fall back on Mac/web)
Two duplicate hero images (Image 5 + Image 6) — investigate whether one is an overlay
Many text bounding boxes are smaller than their content — when rebuilt with native shapes, they will reflow; widths must be increased
Background fill of slide 1 not directly readable via Office.js — confirm by inspecting slideMaster1.xml <p:bg> directly
