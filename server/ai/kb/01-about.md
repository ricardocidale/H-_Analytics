About the Platform

H+ Analytics is a GAAP-compliant financial analytics portal built by Norfolk AI for boutique hotel portfolio management. The platform supports VRBO, STR (short-term rental), and traditional lodge business models with USALI-compliant financials.

The business works on a dual-entity model. On one side you have the individual hotel properties, each held in its own SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) to keep the finances and liability separate. On the other side you have the Management Company. The management company earns its revenue by charging management fees to the properties it manages.

There are two types of management fees:
- A base fee, which is a percentage of each property's total revenue
- An incentive fee, which is a percentage of each property's Gross Operating Profit (but only when the property is actually profitable)

To get started before management fees kicked in, the company raised working capital through funding tranches (such as SAFE — Simple Agreement for Future Equity). These come in multiple rounds and are not counted as revenue — they represent future equity in the company.

The portal is a closed system — only pre-approved users added by an admin can access it. There is no public sign-up. Users are added manually or invited via email by an administrator.
