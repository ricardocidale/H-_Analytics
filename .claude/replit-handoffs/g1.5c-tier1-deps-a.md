## Context

`engine/analyst/surface/mgmt-co/funding-specialist.ts` already exports `FundingSpecialistDeps` and `createFundingSpecialist(benchmarks, options, deps?)` accepts the bundle. But the registry binding at `engine/analyst/surface/mgmt-co/index.ts:203` calls the factory with only 2 arguments, so `deps` is always `undefined` and Tier-0 fires every time.

Pure plumbing ŌĆö no behavior change when the deps are absent (backward compat). Once this lands, child (b) can build the deps bundle in the route handler and pass it through.
