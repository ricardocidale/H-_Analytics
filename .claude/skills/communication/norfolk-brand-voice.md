# Norfolk AI Brand Voice

The parent brand voice that applies to all Norfolk AI products and communications.
Individual products (H+ Analytics, Conversational Platform) layer their own
personality on top of this foundation.

---

## Who Norfolk AI Is

Norfolk AI builds AI agents that embed into specific industries and deliver
intelligence — not just answers. Founded by a 25-year enterprise tech veteran
who saw the same problem everywhere: businesses drown in data but starve for
intelligence.

**The thesis:** Every industry has a version of the same problem. You have
data but no one telling you what it means. The solution isn't more dashboards.
It's agents that think, act, and deliver intelligence within the workflow.

## Writing Voice

Write the way a behavioral economist explains a complex idea at dinner —
simple language, everyday analogies, a gentle wit, and the quiet confidence
that the reader is smart enough to get it.

Show radical attention and respect — see the specific person and their
specific situation, not a generic user.

### Principles

1. **Simple over technical** — if a 10-year-old can't follow the structure
   of the sentence (not the content), it's too complex
2. **Analogies over definitions** — connect new concepts to things people
   already understand
3. **Nudge, don't lecture** — present the better option, don't mandate it
4. **Specificity is credibility** — "8 comparable properties" not "several"
5. **Lead with the verdict** — recommendation first, evidence second
6. **Honest about tradeoffs** — "Control, not cost" not "Everything is amazing"
7. **Empathetic specificity** — name the pain before offering the solution
8. **Accompany, don't instruct** — walk alongside, don't stand above

### AI Terminology

**Proud to say:**
- "AI-powered intelligence"
- "Our AI agents reviewed..."
- "AI-generated ranges backed by market data"
- "Norfolk AI Engine"

**Never say in user-facing text:**
- "The LLM outputted..."
- "Machine learning algorithms..."
- "Vector similarity search..."
- Implementation details of any kind

**The rule:** AI is what we do. How we do it is not the user's concern.

## Visual Identity

### Colors (Noir Executive Theme — primary)
- Background: #0F1117 (near-black, cool blue undertone)
- Gold accent: #C9A84C (CTAs, KPI highlights, active states — never decorative)
- Foreground: #F1F3F5 (warm white)
- Negative: #E54D4D (errors, negative values)
- Positive: #4ADE80 (gains, confirmations)

### Typography
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif authority)
- Body/Data: Inter (clean, high-legibility)
- Financial tables: JetBrains Mono (monospace)

### Design Principles
- Gold is reserved for interactive elements — never decorative fill
- Card glassmorphism with backdrop-blur for layered depth
- Negative values in red with parentheses: ($1,234)
- Skeleton shimmer for loading states, never spinners for AI operations
- Premium financial terminal aesthetic — not consumer SaaS

## Quality Check

Before shipping any Norfolk AI communication, ask:

1. Does it sound like a sharp colleague wrote it? (not software)
2. Would the reader feel respected? (not talked down to)
3. Is there a specific recommendation? (not a menu of options)
4. Is the risk explicit and manageable? (not hidden or scary)
5. Would an investor find this defensible? (not vague)
