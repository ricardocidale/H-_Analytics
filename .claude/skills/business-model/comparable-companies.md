# Comparable Companies

## Ennismore (THE Aspirational Model)
- Multi-brand lifestyle platform merged with Accor (66/34 split)
- F&B at 30-50% of revenue; Mama Shelter close to 50/50
- Restaurants are standalone destinations, not hotel amenities
- Dis-loyalty program: community-first, cross-brand discovery
- Fees: 2-3% base + 8-10% incentive + 1-2% brand + 1-2% marketing
- ~100+ properties, 100+ pipeline

## Nobu Hospitality (Proves 50/50 F&B)
- Restaurant brand preceded hotel brand → F&B at 40-60% of revenue
- Fees: 4-6% brand (higher reflecting restaurant premium) + 2-3% management
- ~15-20 properties. Key: F&B must draw non-hotel guests

## Auberge Resorts (Conversion Specialist)
- Converts estates, ranches, historic properties into luxury resorts
- Backed by Friedkin Group (patient family office capital)
- Fees: 3-4% base + 10-15% incentive + pre-opening fees ($1-3M+)
- ~20-25 properties, grows deliberately (2-4/year)

## Selina (CAUTIONARY TALE for LatAm)
- WHAT WENT WRONG: master leases (fixed costs), grew to 100+ before proving economics, low RevPAR, multi-country complexity, SPAC at $1.2B → bankruptcy
- LESSONS: Use management contracts not leases. Prove at 5-10 before scaling. Premium positioning > budget. Multi-country LatAm is extraordinarily complex.

## Grupo Habita (Closest LatAm Analog)
- Converts unique buildings in Mexico → boutique hotels
- Deep cultural integration. ~10-15 properties over 20+ years.

## Scaling Phases
| Phase | Properties | Time | Fee Income | Margin |
|-------|-----------|------|-----------|--------|
| Prove | 1-5 | 3-5yr | $2-4M | Breakeven |
| Build | 5-15 | 3-5yr | $7-12M | 10-20% |
| Accelerate | 15-30 | 3-5yr | $15-25M | 20-35% |
| Platform | 30-50+ | Ongoing | $25-50M | 30-45% |

**Breakeven: 8-15 managed properties, 3-7 years**

## Valuations
- Early: 2-5x fee revenue / 8-12x EBITDA
- Growth: 6-10x / 12-18x
- Mature: 10-15x / 18-25x
- Brand/franchise income valued HIGHER than management fees
