
#293 - Add Lodge Business Model & Update Lakeview Haven
What & Why
Add "lodge" as a third business model type, positioned between VRBO/STR and Hotel. Lodges are larger STR-style properties (8–20 rooms) that offer hotel-like amenities (gym, sauna, hot tub, sports courts, media rooms) but operate on a whole-property rental basis without F&B departments, events staff, or front desk. Simultaneously, update the Lakeview Haven Lodge seed data and photos with real property information scraped from its actual listing site.

Done looks like
The Business Model selector on the property edit page shows three options: Hotel, Lodge, and VRBO/STR — with Lodge described as something like "Large vacation lodge — whole-property rental, premium amenities, no F&B/events departments"
Lakeview Haven Lodge seed record uses businessModel: "lodge", roomCount: 8, and an accurate description pulled from the real website
Lakeview Haven Lodge seed photos are replaced with ~10–15 curated real photos from the OwnerRez listing (using the uc.orez.io URLs from the site), with descriptive captions from the alt text
The research methodology skill is updated with a Lodge section describing its financial profile (between Hotel and VRBO)
All existing schema validation, research staleness triggers, and UI components accept "lodge" as a valid business model without errors
Out of scope
Implementing separate financial calculation logic branching for lodge vs hotel vs vrbo in the pro forma engine (that can come later)
Updating any other seed properties to use the lodge model
Building new Lodge-specific UI pages or dashboard views
