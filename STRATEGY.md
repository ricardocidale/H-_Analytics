# H+ Analytics Strategy

## Target problem

Boutique hospitality asset managers and their investor groups have no purpose-built tool for modeling multi-property acquisition and transformation pipelines — building USALI-compliant pro formas in Excel takes months and a team of researchers, and produces nothing LP-ready. The combination of institutional-grade financial modeling, AI-driven market research, and investor-presentation output does not exist anywhere else in a single product built for this domain.

## Our approach

H+ bets on making institutional-grade hospitality analysis self-serve — pairing a guided, structured workflow with an AI research layer and embedded range guidance so that an unsophisticated investor can safely produce USALI-compliant scenarios and LP-ready reports without needing analysts or finance expertise. We target investors who identify with experience- and community-based hospitality, so the brand flywheel compounds: early LP communities attract the next wave of properties and investors.

## Who it's for

**Primary:** Boutique hospitality LP / angel investor — evaluating whether to back a specific property transformation (commercial → boutique hotel or STR), targeting 20%+ IRR, not a finance professional. They're hiring H+ to run credible scenarios and produce LP-ready materials without an analyst on staff.

**Secondary:** Management company investor — betting on the brand platform scaling from a handful of properties to dozens or hundreds. They're hiring H+ to understand the management co financials, fee structure, and long-term growth trajectory.

## Key metrics

- **Capital raised** — total capital committed to properties and management co; tracked deal-by-deal; the primary signal now
- **Scenarios completed and shared** — proxy for active investor engagement; measured in DB
- **Slide decks generated and downloaded** — measures whether LP-ready output is reaching decision-makers; DB
- **Active investors per org** — measures whether scenario sharing and collaboration is working; DB
- **Properties at or above projected IRR** — lagging, post-investment operational signal; tracked deal-by-deal

## Tracks

### Financial engine accuracy

Maintaining and extending the USALI-compliant projection engine, verification system, and assumption input system (range badges, infotips, help text) so outputs are trustworthy enough to put in front of LPs.

_Why it serves the approach:_ The guided workflow only works if the underlying numbers are correct and defensible — this is the trust layer.

### AI research depth

Extending the orchestrated specialist system (market research, comp sets, funding analysis, reference brands) so investors get research-grade intelligence without hiring researchers.

_Why it serves the approach:_ This is the intern-replacement layer — the part that makes months of Excel work take hours.

### Investor workflow and presentation

Polishing the end-to-end investor journey: scenario creation, sharing, PPTX slide deck generation, financial report export, and Rebecca co-pilot guidance.

_Why it serves the approach:_ The product's value is only realized when an unsophisticated investor can walk through it independently and share the output with confidence.

### Brand and community go-to-market

Identifying and engaging experience- and community-based hospitality verticals (specific interest groups, travel communities) as both investor sources and property pipelines.

_Why it serves the approach:_ The branding flywheel — early community investors attract properties to the brand, which attracts more investors, compounding without traditional deal sourcing.
